agregarIncentivos contributivos y gestoría

  • Descripción

    The new Incentives Code provides fiscal benefits for activities developed in specific areas such as the special development zone of the Traditional Urban Center and the Special Development Corridors, among others which due to their potential for growth and their impact on the economy as a whole are considered a priority. In addition, it provides benefits to more than 21 types of eligible units or businesses.

  • Costo de Solicitud

    200

  • Informacion Adicional

    Disqualifying factors: companies already established in the city that are not planning to grow.

  • Nombre De Agencia

    Secretaria de Desarrollo Económico de Caguas
